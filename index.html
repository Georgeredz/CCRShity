Process

Simple. Fast. Transparent.

Report a cleanliness issue in your neighborhood in under two minutes — and track its resolution in real time.

1

Submit a Report

Select the issue type, add the location, describe the problem, and optionally attach photos from your phone.

2

Officials Review

Barangay officials are notified instantly. They review the report and assign it a priority status.

3

Issue Resolved

Track progress from pending to in-progress to resolved. Receive confirmation when the problem is fixed.

Features

Everything Your Barangay Needs

A complete civic reporting platform built specifically for Philippine communities.

Instant Submission

Submit reports directly from your phone or computer in under two minutes with photo attachments.

Live Status Tracking

Monitor every report from submission to resolution with real-time status updates visible to all residents.

Admin Dashboard

Barangay officials get a powerful management interface to filter, assign, and update all incoming reports.

SMS & Viber Notifications

Get notified through SMS or Viber -- the most effective communication channels at the local level in the Philippines.

Anonymous Reporting

Report sensitive issues like illegal dumping anonymously. Your identity stays hidden while you still get a tracking code.

Resolution Verification

When officials mark an issue resolved, reporters get 48 hours to confirm or re-open with new photo evidence.
